Locations of all car club lots (i.e. pick-up/drop-off locations) in the UK
Data are scraped from the interactive car club location map on the CoMo website: 
https://como.org.uk/shared-mobility/shared-cars/where/
Year: 2020

location - name/address of car club lot 
lon, lat - coordinates of lot
number_of_vehicles - number of car club vehicles stationed at the lot
operator - operator of car club lot (5 in total: Co-Wheels, Enterprise Car Club, Ubeeqo, Zipcar, Zipcar Flex)
lad - code of the local authority district (lad) that the lot belongs to
lad_name - corresponding lad name
lsoa_name - name of lower super output area that the lot belongs to
